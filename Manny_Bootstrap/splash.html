Champions

The champions of the League of Justice are as diverse as they are powerful, all of them bringing unique styles and abilities into the Institute of War. Each fighting for their own political faction, they bring abilities and fighting styles from around Valoran into the arena to resolve conflicts and of course, entertain.

Items

Each player can choose a variety of champion varying in many different roles, each having their own unique characteristics and abilities, with all champions starting off each game fairly weak and progressing by accumulating gold and experience over the course of the game. As each player accumulate gold, they can use it on items that boost their champion's statistics that enhances various abilities.

Choose your items wisely!
